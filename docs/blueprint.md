# **App Name**: TOTO

## Core Features:

- User Authentication & Profiles: Secure OTP-based login and registration for both riders and drivers, allowing each to manage their respective profiles.
- Interactive Ride Booking: Seamless selection of pick-up and drop-off locations using Google Maps API, enabling riders to request a ride for e-rickshaws or cabs.
- Real-time Ride Status & Tracking: Provides live tracking of the driver's location for riders and offers navigation updates for drivers using Google Maps integration.
- Driver Onboarding & Vehicle Registration: A dedicated workflow for drivers to register, input vehicle details (e-rickshaw/cab), and securely upload identity documents (e.g., Aadhaar/PAN) for verification.
- Driver Ride Management Panel: An intuitive interface for drivers to accept or decline ride requests, view trip details, and update ride status throughout the journey.
- Fare Estimation & Transaction View: Calculates and displays estimated ride fares to riders before booking, and provides a basic view of past transactions for both users and drivers.
- AI-Powered Driver Communication Assistant: An AI tool to generate quick, contextual communication messages, helping drivers efficiently communicate with riders during a trip (e.g., 'Arriving shortly,' 'Waiting at pickup point').

## Style Guidelines:

- The chosen color scheme revolves around a clean 'Orange and White' theme. A bright, energetic orange, such as #FFA500, will serve as the primary color for interactive elements, branding, and call-to-actions, evoking a modern and approachable feel. This is complemented by a crisp white (#FFFFFF) for main content areas, ensuring excellent readability and a lightweight, open aesthetic. Subtle shades of light grey (#F0F0F0) can be used for backgrounds of secondary content blocks or dividers to add depth without clutter, maintaining the app's simple and modern design.
- For both headlines and body text, the 'Inter' sans-serif typeface is recommended. Its clean, objective, and modern aesthetic aligns perfectly with the 'Uber and Ola' feel, ensuring high legibility and a contemporary look for all textual elements in the app, suitable for various content types from trip details to user profiles.
- Use clear, universally recognizable icons related to transportation, user actions, and location. Icons should be line-based or subtly filled to maintain a 'lightweight' and modern appearance, enhancing usability without clutter.
- Adopt a clean, intuitive layout with a focus on simplicity and ease of use. Utilize card-based designs for ride details and profile information, generous whitespace, and clear hierarchical arrangements to guide users seamlessly through the app, mirroring the modern user experience of leading ride-hailing platforms.
- Implement subtle, swift animations for transitions between screens, button interactions, and real-time map updates. Animations should be fluid and efficient, contributing to a premium, responsive feel without introducing unnecessary delays, reinforcing the 'lightweight' requirement.
